Our Speciallization

  • PLC Pannels

    Suitable for Process Industries, Interlocking of breakers with fool-proof arrangement, Load-dependent close/trip of breakers and start/stop of DG Sets.

  • Power Control Centres

    Dedicated power distribution, control, ground-fault protection, monitoring and alarm panel, Consists of an assembled circuit-breaker panel board, Supported by CAD Design.

  • Automatic Power Factor Control Pannels

    Perfect Compartmentalized construction, Made of 14/16 SWG CRCA Sheet, Electro statically applied Polyester, Designed with dust and vermin proof modules.

  • Motor Control Centres

    We are a trusted French pool tables manufacturers and supplier. The French pool tables increase seating capacity. They are made fro the best quality hardwood

  • synchronizing Panels

    Synchronizing different DG sets according to the load requirement of the system based on load in KW, Software enabled implementation of Change in scheme saves time and resources..

  • AMF Pannels

    Facilitates automatic changeover from mains to standby Generator during power failure and shifts load to mains, after voltage stabilizes, when power returns with proper cooling time for DG.

  • Fire Fighting Pannels

    Act automatically sensing a drop in air-pressure due to rise in atmospheric temperature thus keeping the Fire-Fighting Pumps always on alert to face any eventuality.

  • Metering Pannels

    Metering panels are electrical enclosures housing meters to monitor and manage energy consumption. They measure parameters like voltage, current crucial for effective energy management.

  • Feedars Pillars & Weather-Proof Kiosks

    Feedars Pillars & Weather-Proof Kiosks: Sturdy, adaptable structures for outdoor settings, facilitating food distribution, equipped with weather-resistant materials.

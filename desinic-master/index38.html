Metering Pannels:

• Metering panels are used to accurately measure and monitor the consumption of   electricity within a building or facility.
• Metering panels provide essential data for billing purposes, allowing utility companies or   property managers to accurately charge customers or tenants based on their actual   energy usage.
• Metering panels help in analyzing energy consumption patterns, identifying areas of high   usage, and implementing energy-saving measures.
• Metering panels play a crucial role in ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements   and standards for energy usage and reporting.
•Metering panels provide valuable data for performance monitoring of electrical systems   and equipment, enabling proactive maintenanc.

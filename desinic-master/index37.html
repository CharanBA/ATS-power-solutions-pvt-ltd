Fire Fighting Panel:

• Act automatically sensing a drop in air-pressure due to rise in atmospheric temperature   thus keeping the Fire-Fighting Pumps always on alert to face any eventuality.
• Specially designed to facilitate easy installation and maintenance.
• Available in floor-mounting and wall-mounting modes.
• Comprises of Automatic Diesel Engine Starter, battery charger with protections such as    HWT, LOP, etc. SFU/MCCB/HRC backup used as Incomers/Outgoings.
• Audio/Visual alarm when Hydrant goes ON.
• Busbar system for higher HP pump-sets.
